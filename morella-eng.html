Morella

Project information

  • Category: Software development
  • Client: Bicon S.A.
  • Project initial date: March 2022
  • Project status: In prodution
  • Languages: Python and SQL

Morella is a management system developed to meet the needs of Bicon S.A., a company located in Rosario dedicated to the administration of cementeries.
The software was born with the intention of replacing the previous one, developed in 1989 with Cobol, so it was decided to continue using Windows console as user interface. Therefore, through an extremely simple and intuitive interface, a robust app is achieved, capable of satisfying all the company's need in a simple and improved way
With Morella you can do payments, cash movement registration, clients account statement and communication with them.
